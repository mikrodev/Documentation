Installation Information

Rail Installation

DIN Rail Mountage

First, the upper part of the device is mounted on the DIN rail. Then, with the help of the springs behind the device, when a lightly force is applied to the lower part, the device locates into the DIN rail easily and the montage is completed.

DIN Rail Demountage

To demount the device, firstly it is pulled from the bottom using flexibility of the spring, the device is removed from the DIN Rail and the demounting is completed.

Expansion Installation

The DM100 product and its extensions are mounted by sliding over the rail in such a way that the connectors correponds.
