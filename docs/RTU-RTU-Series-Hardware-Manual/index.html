Mounting Information

RTU 300 Series Hardware Manual

DIN Rail Mountage

First, the upper part of the device is mounted on the DIN rail. Then, with the help of the springs behind the device, when a lightly force is applied to the lower part, the device locates into the DIN rail easily and the montage is completed.

Expansion Installation

Assembly between RTU300 series RTU products and XIO211 series expansion modules is carried out by sliding the tabs over the rail so that the tabs overlap each other
