XIO110 Expansion Modules

DIN Rail Mountingâ€‹

First, the upper part of the device is mounted on the DIN rail. Then, with the help of the springs behind the device, when a lightly force is applied to the lower part, the device locates into the DIN rail easily and the montage is completed.

Expansion Installationâ€‹

Assembly between MP110 series PLC or RIO110 series remote I/O products and XIO110 series expansion modules is carried out by sliding the tabs over the rail so that the tabs overlap each other.
